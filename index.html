Overview

ECE 6950 is a PhD level course that investigates the emergence of AI-driven performance engineering for computer systems. These are our learning objectives:

  • Get up to date with the latest research on AI-driven systems research.
  • Develop advanced understanding of agentic systems.
  • Identify unsolved systems efficiency problems.
  • Solve a systems efficiency problem with AI.
